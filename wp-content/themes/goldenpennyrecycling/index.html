About Us

Golden penny Enterprise is a large Environmental depot and scrap metal processing facility. We work with Divert NS which is a Not-for-profit Corporation working in partnership with Nova Scotians to improve the province environment and economy through recycling and waste diversion. We process cans/ bottles/ plastics through RRFB program . The cans/bottles and plastics are reprocessed and manufactured into new Bottles/ Cans and Plastics. This saves raw materials and energy. It decreases the landfill site, pollution and keeps the Earth Green.
